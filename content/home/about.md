I'm a therapeutic social worker with a Master's degree in Social Work, a Bachelor's degree in Journalism, a trauma-informed yoga teacher, former personal trainer, and health coach, with almost 20 years of professional experience supporting individuals, families, children, and young people through complexity, change, and emotional life.

My work brings together social work, psychological understanding, developmental and relational insight, body-based awareness, and lived cross-cultural experience.
